What is LAG-ID?

LAG-ID is a Decentralized Financial Infrastructure for the simulation industry. It utilizes a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Swarm network to transform your achievements into verifiable digital assets.

By using Ed25519 cryptography and the Iroh networking stack, LAG-ID ensures that your identity and your Diesels (Ð) exist independently of any central server. You are the sovereign master of your data.

The Big FAQ

Is it safe to send and receive Diesels (Ð)? Yes. Every transaction is a Cryptographically Signed Payload. When you receive a .diesel file, the Swarm network validates the issuer's signature and verifies the asset's serial number against the global ledger. It is physically impossible to forge a valid transaction.
What happens if I lose my phone? Can I recover my money? Because LAG-ID is decentralized, your "money" lives on the Global Swarm Ledger. If you have your LAGID_BACKUP.json file, you can restore your identity on a new device. Once synced, the network will automatically recognize your past transactions and restore your balance. Without your backup, the private keys are lost forever.
Can I send Diesels while I am driving in the desert with no 5G? Absolutely. The protocol is Offline-First. You can generate a signed transaction file even with zero signal. You can then hand that file over via Bluetooth or any local transfer. The receiver's app will accept the asset into its local vault immediately, and the transaction will broadcast to the Swarm network the next time a connection is established.
Can I "double-spend" by restoring an old backup? No. The Swarm network maintains a Nullifier Registry. When you spend Diesels, the unique serial number of that asset is marked as "Consumed" by the P2P nodes. If you try to restore an old backup and spend the same money again, the network will reject the transaction because that serial is already dead in the global consensus.
Who controls the exchange rate of Diesels? The community. Diesels are Simulation Utility Assets. Their value is determined by the goods, services, and payrolls managed by the VTC owners and pilots within the Convoyrama ecosystem.

Security Architecture

Industrial-Grade Protection

1. Zero-Knowledge PIN: Your PIN is used as entropy to derive your keys via 100,000 PBKDF2 iterations. It is never stored on disk.

2. AES-GCM Encryption: Your private keys are stored in an encrypted bunker on your device. Even if your phone is rooted, the keys remain unreadable without your PIN.

3. Volatile RAM: Once unlocked, your "Live Key" only exists in protected system memory. It is purged the moment the session ends.

Common Errors

"Network Anchor Timeout" Your device cannot establish a connection to the Swarm Relay. Ensure your internet is active and that your local firewall permits P2P Traffic.
"Invalid Integrity Seal" The local vault detects an attempt to manually modify its internal ledger. For your protection, the wallet is locked. Please restore your LAGID_BACKUP.json to resume operations.
