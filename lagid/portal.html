User Manual

1. Welcome & Onboarding

Start by connecting your Steam Account. This acts as an anchor to verify your seniority in SCS games. You will then create a 6-digit Master PIN. IMPORTANT: We do not store this PIN. If you lose it, your data is gone.

2. Identity Hub

Your main dashboard. Here you can see your Sovereign Identity Card. Tap it to see it in full screen. Use the "Profile" button to complete your driver data, upload your avatar, and link your social media.

3. Fleet Hub (VTC)

Manage your affiliation. If you belong to a VTC, your statistics and rank will be verified by the protocol. Owners can manage payrolls and collective investments.

4. The Wallet

Manage your Diesels (Ð). You can send assets via QR or NodeID. Every transaction generates a digital receipt signed with your private key.

5. Identity Designer

Customize your visual manifest. Change background templates, adjust colors (Chroma Engine), and choose which data is visible to the Swarm. Committing a design generates an encrypted PNG asset.

The Extended FAQ

What are Diesels (Ð)? Diesels are Utility Assets used to reward merit and participation. They are not "money" in the legal sense. They are spent on digital assets, vanity items, and community services within the LAG-ID network.
Is my personal data being sold? Never. Because we don't have a central database, there is no data to sell. Your profile is broadcasted to the Swarm only when you choose to share it. We don't even know your email.
Why do I need a PIN? The PIN is the "key" to the vault. Without it, your private Ed25519 keys cannot be derived. This ensures that only someone with physical access to your phone AND knowledge of the PIN can sign transactions.
Can I use the app on multiple phones? Yes, but you must manually transfer your LAGID_BACKUP.json file. Since there is no "Cloud Login", you are responsible for moving your identity between devices.

FAQ Extendida

¿Se venden mis datos personales? Nunca. Debido a que no tenemos una base de datos central, no hay datos que vender. Tu perfil se emite al Enjambre solo cuando decides compartirlo. Ni siquiera conocemos tu email.
¿Por qué necesito un PIN? El PIN es la "llave" de la bóveda. Sin él, tus llaves privadas Ed25519 no pueden ser derivadas. Esto asegura que solo alguien con acceso físico a tu teléfono Y conocimiento del PIN pueda firmar transacciones.

Security Protocols

The Triple Shield

1. Hardware Guard: We use the Android Keystore to encrypt the vault at the hardware level.

2. Signature Guard: Every action is signed using Ed25519. It is mathematically impossible to spoof your identity.

3. Privacy Guard: We utilize Zero-Knowledge principles. Your secrets stay secret.

Protocolos de Seguridad

El Triple Escudo

1. Hardware Guard: Usamos el Keystore de Android para cifrar la bóveda a nivel de hardware.

2. Signature Guard: Cada acción está firmada usando Ed25519. Es matemáticamente imposible suplantar tu identidad.

3. Privacy Guard: Utilizamos principios de Zero-Knowledge. Tus secretos se quedan en secreto.
