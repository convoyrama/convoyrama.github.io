Extended FAQ

Where is my data stored? Only on your phone. LAG-ID uses a Local-First architecture. We do not have servers, databases, or cloud logins. You are the only person who has the keys to your information.
What happens if I delete the app? Your data is deleted from the device. To recover it, you MUST have a manual backup of your LAGID_BACKUP.json and remember your Master PIN.
Why do I need to connect Steam? Steam acts as an Oracle of Merit. It allows the protocol to verify that you are a real driver with real SCS game time, preventing the creation of fake "bot" identities.

Preguntas Frecuentes (FAQ)

¿Dónde se guardan mis datos? Solo en tu teléfono. LAG-ID utiliza una arquitectura Local-First. No tenemos servidores, bases de datos ni logins en la nube. Tú eres la única persona con las llaves de tu información.
¿Qué pasa si borro la aplicación? Tus datos se eliminan del dispositivo. Para recuperarlos, DEBES tener una copia manual de tu LAGID_BACKUP.json y recordar tu PIN Maestro.
¿Por qué conectar Steam? Steam actúa como un Oráculo de Mérito. Permite al protocolo verificar que eres un conductor real con tiempo de juego legítimo en SCS, evitando identidades falsas o bots.

The Diesel Standard (Ð)

Utility, not Currency

Diesels (Ð) are utility assets designed to reward verified participation. They have ZERO real-world monetary value. You cannot buy them with fiat, and you cannot sell them for fiat.

Diesels are earned through merit (driving hours, achievements, VTC service) and are spent on digital services, vanity items, and reputation upgrades within the Convoyrama ecosystem.

Security: The Triple Shield

1. Hardware Guard

Encryption keys are managed by the Android Keystore. They never leave the hardware security module of your phone.

2. Blinded Internal Logic

The protocol enforces security rules (Time, Network, Vault) at the core level. The UI is just a read-only mirror of this hardened logic.

3. Ed25519 P2P Tunnel

Communications between drivers are signed and encrypted. No middleman can intercept or forge your messages.
