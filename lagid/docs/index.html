The Sovereign Manifesto

"If the users don't control the program, the program controls the users." — Richard Stallman

1. Why does LAG-ID exist?

LAG-ID was born from a shared frustration: the stagnation of large simulation platforms. For years, we have seen how massive communities have remained frozen in time, limited by central structures that do not evolve or that prioritize control over user experience.

We wanted a more complete, more roleplay-focused experience. We wanted VTCs to have real tools to manage their drivers, reward merit, and build their own economy without asking anyone for permission. Since no one did it, we decided to do it ourselves.

2. What is LSP (LAG Sovereign Protocol)?

LSP is the engine behind LAG-ID. It is a high-performance **Rust-based** protocol designed for the decentralized trucking world. Unlike traditional apps that rely on a central server, LSP treats every driver's phone as a "Node". These nodes talk directly to each other using the **Iroh Swarm** engine, creating an unstoppable network where the community is the owner.

3. GNU Philosophy: Your Identity is Yours

LAG-ID is governed under the principles of Free Software. This is not just a matter of price; it is a matter of Liberty. In this protocol, the user is the absolute sovereign.

Our "Golden Rule" of Privacy:

We do not collect your data. We do not handle your statistics. We do not have a central database. There is no "Cloud Login". We sell nothing.

LAG-ID is a tool that lives on your device. Your information is **blinded** to us. We provide the technical chassis, but you own the data.
